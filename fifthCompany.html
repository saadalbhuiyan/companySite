Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our research-driven approach and services.

How do you ensure intellectual property protection?

All projects are protected under strict NDAs and confidentiality agreements. We implement isolated VPCs with comprehensive audit logging for high-security clients. Our enterprise clients can also request on-premise deployment and dedicated team isolation to ensure complete IP protection.

What is your technology stack and methodology?

We use cutting-edge technologies including Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, FastAPI, React, Next.js, Kubernetes, Terraform, and both GCP & AWS. Our methodology combines rigorous research validation with agile development practices. We always choose the best tool for each specific job rather than being locked into a single stack.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we provide 30 days of free bug fixes and technical support with all projects. Beyond that, we offer flexible monthly retainer options for ongoing monitoring, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include 24/7 SRE support.

What makes your research validation process unique?

Our PhD team conducts comprehensive literature reviews, statistical validation, and reproducibility testing before any development begins. We replicate key experiments, validate methodologies, and ensure all assumptions are scientifically sound. This approach reduces technical risk and ensures your product is built on proven foundations.

How do you handle compliance and security requirements?

We maintain SOC-2 Type II compliance and can deliver FedRAMP and ISO 27001 ready solutions. Our infrastructure includes end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance monitoring. For enterprise clients, we provide dedicated security assessments and can work within your existing compliance frameworks.
